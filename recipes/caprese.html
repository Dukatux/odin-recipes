Caprese Salad (Insalata Caprese)

Description

This is an extremely simple and fresh Italian salad, often served as an appetizer. Its colours—red, white, and green—represent the Italian flag.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Mozzarella cheese (Mozzarella di bufala or Fior di latte), sliced
  • Ripe tomatoes (preferably heirloom or beefsteak), sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO)
  • Balsamic glaze (optional, but highly recommended)
  • Salt (preferably sea salt)
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice Ingredients: Slice the tomatoes and the fresh mozzarella into evenly thick rounds.
  2. Arrange: On a serving platter or plate, alternate the slices of tomato and mozzarella, placing a fresh basil leaf between each slice (or simply scatter the basil leaves over the arrangement).
  3. Season: Drizzle the entire salad generously with Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
  4. Final Touches: Sprinkle with salt and black pepper to taste. If desired, lightly drizzle a balsamic glaze over the top just before serving.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately at room temperature to enjoy the fresh flavors.
