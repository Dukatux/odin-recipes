Spaghetti Aglio e Olio (Garlic and Oil Pasta)

Description

This is a traditional, quick, and simple Neapolitan dish that focuses on the quality of the olive oil and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (about 8 oz / 225g)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO)
  • Garlic (4-6 cloves), thinly sliced
  • Red pepper flakes (Peperoncino) (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt (for the pasta water)
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente (firm to the bite). Before draining, reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water.
  2. Infuse the Oil: While the pasta cooks, heat the EVOO in a large skillet over low heat. Add the thinly sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© the garlic slowly until it is fragrant and lightly golden (do not let it burn, or it will taste bitter).
  3. Combine: Add the drained spaghetti directly into the skillet with the oil and garlic. Toss well.
  4. Emulsify: Add a splash or two of the reserved pasta water to the skillet. Toss rapidly; the starch in the water will help the oil and water emulsify, creating a light, shiny sauce that clings to the pasta.
  5. Finish: Stir in the chopped fresh parsley. Serve immediately, topped with optional Parmesan cheese.
