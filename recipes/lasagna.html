Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made of stacked layers of pasta sheets (lasagne), alternating with fillings such as meat sauce (ragù), Béchamel sauce, and a mix of cheeses (typically mozzarella and ricotta or Parmesan). It's a rich, hearty, and satisfying casserole often characterized by its bubbling, golden-brown top.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • The Meat Sauce (Ragù)
  • The Béchamel or Ricotta Layer
  • The Cheeses

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce (Ragù)
  2. Prepare the Noodles
  3. Make the Cheese or Béchamel Layer
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake
