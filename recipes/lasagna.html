Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made of stacked layers of pasta sheets (lasagne), alternating with fillings such as meat sauce (ragù), Béchamel sauce, and a mix of cheeses (typically mozzarella and ricotta or Parmesan). It's a rich, hearty, and satisfying casserole often characterized by its bubbling, golden-brown top.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
    • Lasagna noodles/sheets (fresh or dried)
  • The Meat Sauce (Ragù)
    • Ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
    • Onion, diced
    • Garlic, minced
    • Crushed tomatoes (or tomato purée)
    • Tomato paste
    • Italian seasoning (or dried oregano and basil)
    • Olive oil
    • Salt and black pepper
  • Béchamel Sauce (often used in authentic Italian recipes)
    • Butter
    • All-purpose flour
    • Milk
    • Nutmeg (a pinch)
    • Salt
  • The Cheeses
    • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • Parmesan cheese (Parmigiano Reggiano), grated

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce (Ragù)
    1. Sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
    2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  2. Prepare the Noodles
    1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions (if using dried pasta). Drain and set aside.
  3. Make the Cheese or Béchamel Layer
    1. If using Ricotta: Mix the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
    2. If using Bechamel: Melt the butter, whisk in the flour (to make a roux), and gradually add the milk until thickened. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    2. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce.
    3. Top with a layer of the ricotta mix or Bechamel sauce.
    4. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    5. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta/Bechamel, cheese) until the dish is full, finishing with a generous layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake
    1. Bake in a preheated oven ($180^\circ\text{C}$ or $350^\circ\text{F}$) for about 45-60 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbling and the top is golden-brown.
    2. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
